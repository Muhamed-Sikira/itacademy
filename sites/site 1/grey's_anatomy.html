Station 19

Gray's Anatomy is an American series that has won awards Emmy and a Golden Globe . It appeared on American small screens as a seasonal replacement on March 27, 2005 , following the popular " Housewives ."

The series is based on the life of Dr. Meredith Gray, who begins working as an intern at Seattle Grace Fiction Hospital in Seattle , Washington . The series also deals with the lives of other characters, Meredith's friends, and the role of the narrator is played by Meredith herself, at the beginning and end of the episode. The title of the series is an allusion to the anatomy textbook "Gray's Anatomy ". The series has been renewed for its 10th season which premiered on September 26, 2013.
